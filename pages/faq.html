FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Can I select more than one package?

One is only allowed to be on one package.
Purchasing more than one package nullifies the previous package and
moves you to the recent package purchased

2. Can I book a ride for a friend?

Yes, you are allowed to book for a friend.
However, we encourage that every user creates
their own account for safety and security issues

3. If I book a bus and fail to show up at the pick up location, will I be refunded?.

Should you for some reason miss the bus, you will not be refunded since your seat will not be occupied.

4.What are the acceptable payment methods?

For each booking, payments have to be made upfront via mobile money through the paybill number provided
